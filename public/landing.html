FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Seocheck.pro.

What aspects of SEO does Seocheck.pro analyze?

We provide a comprehensive analysis covering meta tags (title, description), headings, image alt text, robots.txt, sitemap presence, HTTPS status, mobile-friendliness, page load speed, content readability, and keyword usage.

How does the AI integration work?

Seocheck.pro utilizes multiple AI models (like Claude, Gemini, OpenAI, etc.) to enhance the analysis. AI helps generate actionable recommendations, summarize findings, evaluate content quality, and provide context-specific suggestions.

Is Seocheck.pro suitable for beginners?

Yes! While powerful enough for professionals, the reports are designed to be understandable, and the recommendations are actionable, making it great for users new to SEO. AI-powered explanations can also help users learn.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation is required. Seocheck.pro is a web-based tool. You just need to enter the URL of the website you want to analyze.

Can I save my analysis reports?

Yes, registered users can access their historical analysis reports through their profile page, allowing them to track progress over time.

What makes Seocheck.pro different from other tools?

Our key differentiator is the strategic integration of multiple AI models for deeper insights and more relevant recommendations, combined with a comprehensive set of core SEO checks in an easy-to-use interface.
