FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BoilerPlate.Help.

How does BoilerPlate.Help create personalized recipes?

Our AI analyzes your dietary preferences, ingredient availability, and past recipe ratings to create personalized recipes that match your taste profile and nutritional needs.

Can I use BoilerPlate.Help if I have dietary restrictions?

BoilerPlate.Help excels at creating recipes for specific dietary needs including vegan, vegetarian, keto, paleo, gluten-free, dairy-free, and accommodating food allergies.

How accurate are the nutritional calculations?

Our nutritional calculations are based on standard USDA data and are typically within 10% accuracy. Premium and Chef plans offer more detailed nutritional breakdowns.

Can I import my existing recipes?

Yes! You can import recipes from websites or by uploading photos of written recipes. Our AI will digitize and organize them in your personal recipe collection.

How does the meal planning feature work?

Our AI creates weekly meal plans based on your preferences, schedule, and nutritional goals. You can customize the number of meals, serving sizes, and even plan for leftovers.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, BoilerPlate.Help is available on iOS and Android devices. The mobile app includes all features of the web version plus barcode scanning for easy ingredient tracking.
